What is Liss cardio?
What is Liss cardio? Liss Cardio (Low-Intensity Steady State) is a form of cardio with low intensity and steady state. Instead of performing strenuous exercises, Liss focuses on gentle and continuous exercises over a long period of time. Practitioners maintain their heart rate at 50-70% of maximum heart rate, in complete contrast to high-intensity exercises like Hiit or Tabata. Popular Liss cardio exercises include: walking, jogging, cycling, stair climbing and swimming. According to experts, just by regularly practicing Liss cardio for 20-30 minutes per session, your body will gradually change positively, from a toned physique to increased endurance.
